Overview

Three unforgettable days in Tbilisi and mountainous parts of Georgia where anyone can find their own way to take a rest.

Itinerary

1 Arrival Day

Arrival at Tbilisi International Airport. Transfer to a hotel.

2 Tbilisi

Old Tbilisi: Bridge of Peace – a bow-shaped illuminated pedestrian glass bridge - examples of contemporary Tbilisi architecture with breathtaking views of the Old Town; Rike Park – a public recreation area from where the cable car crosses the river Mtkvari to the 4th-century fortress of Narikala, overlooking the historical center of the city; “Kartlis Deda”- Mother of Georgia - one of the most recognized symbols of the country; A Brick Mosque with eight angled Minaret from where the Waterfall of Legvtakhevi and The Bridge of Love lay in a few feet; Sulfur Bathrooms in Abanotubani - famed as a source of the city’s existence. Its water full of natural therapeutic minerals springs from the ground at about 40o C. Restaurant - Free time to get lunch in the Georgian, European or Asian restaurant. Mtatsminda - Taking the funicular up to the Mtatsminda Park where plenty of attractions, cafes, and restaurant are available, with great views on Tbilisi. Overnight in Tbilisi.

3 Borjomi

Transfer from Tbilisi to Borjomi; On the road enjoy views of peaceful forests, wild rivers, peaks, and valleys, ancient temples; The Mineral Water Park - dating back to 1850 and see the source of Borjomi’s uncial Warm Mineral Spring Water; You can try unique Pine-tree chewing gums as well as Honey degustation; find plenty of attractions, cafes, and restaurant, outdoor stands selling local honey, jams, and earthenware; Borjomi Cable Car - reach the peak of the Water Park Mountain and take a tour in the forests of the mountain with golf cars to see Borjomi Waterfall; There will be some free time for you get lunch in the Georgian or Asian restaurant; Overnight in Tbilisi

4 Kazbegi

Transfer from Tbilisi to Kazbegi. Driving up the Georgian Military Highway, visiting Jinvali water reservoir to take amazing photos and if the water is low enough, see the remains of an old village under the water. The Ananuri 16th century Complex - on the Aragvi River with stone walls three churches, reservoir, towers and old prison. Next to The area boating and paddling is available. Gudauri ski resort (2200m) and Gudauri Panorama - paragliding is available in summer as well as riding Motorbikes and horses. Free time to get lunch - sheep shish-kebab in the local restaurant. Gudauri Mineral Waters - colored rock geologic formation made due to few mineral springs there, just by the road. Stepantsminda - a valley town with Holy Trinity Church on the hilltop as well as Gveleti waterfall in the Dariali Gorge. Overnight in Tbilisi.

5 Departure

Checking out and transfer to the Tbilisi International Airport
